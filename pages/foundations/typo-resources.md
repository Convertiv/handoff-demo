## Typography Resources

Here are some useful resources for exploring and downloading typography assets:

### Font Libraries

- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/)  
  A comprehensive, free and open source font library supporting variable fonts and web performance.
- [Adobe Fonts](https://fonts.adobe.com/)  
  Premium font library available with Adobe Creative Cloud subscriptions.
- [Fontshare](https://www.fontshare.com/)  
  Free, professional-grade typefaces for designers and developers.
- [Font Squirrel](https://www.fontsquirrel.com/)  
  Free fonts, hand-selected for commercial use.

### Useful Guides

- [Google Fonts Knowledge](https://fonts.google.com/knowledge)  
  Learn about typography fundamentals, font pairing, and legibility.
- [TypeWolf](https://www.typewolf.com/)  
  Trends, recommendations, and inspiration for font pairings.

### Tools

- [Font Pair](https://fontpair.co/)  
  Curated Google Fonts pairs to help you find harmonious typeface combinations.
- [Google Webfonts Helper](https://gwfh.mranftl.com/)  
  Easily self-host Google Fonts with webfont generator.

### Download System/Brand Fonts

> If your project uses Google Fonts, ensure you are loading the correct weights and styles recommended in the [Typography Guidelines](/foundations/typo-guidelines).  
> For proprietary or licensed brand fonts, follow your organization's process to obtain appropriate files and licensing.
